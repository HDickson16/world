Hello, world! My name is Haley.

This webpage is all about me. My likes, dislikes, hopes, and dreams.

Biography

I have a huge passion for the arts, anykind really but I especially have a love for digital and the performing arts. Ever since I was a little kid I would always create what ever came up in my mind, and I still do it to this day. I am just so facinated by how someone can just think of an idea and make it. I take huge inspiration from many artists, such as, Bob Ross, Jamie Hewllet. Someday I hope to reach my goal as a well known artist and make a footprint in the Art World.

In my spare time, when I'm not at school. I love to hang around with my two cats, Lennon and Avery. Their very funny cats and they follow me around the house. They help me relax and feel comforted when I feel stressed or under some type of pressure. As you can tell already I am a huge cat lover, I think their one of the cutest things on the planet. my whole life I've had some kind of cat and they all have a place in my heart. When I draw laying in bed, my cats come to lay with me or fall asleep. When I create what I love, like my art, I always like to have the my side.
